Winter service

Our winter service ensures your safety and the unrestricted usability of your traffic areas during the cold season. With our reliable and professional service, we guarantee prompt and thorough snow removal as well as the gritting of pavements, driveways, parking lots and other affected areas.

Our team is available around the clock to respond quickly to winter weather conditions. Thanks to continuous weather monitoring, we are able to take preventive measures and prevent dangerous icy conditions at an early stage. We rely on state-of-the-art clearing equipment and environmentally friendly gritting agents to offer an effective and sustainable solution.

In addition, we offer individual support and tailor-made solutions for private, commercial and public spaces. With our comprehensive service, you can be sure that your property will remain accessible and safe at all times, even in snow and ice – without any stress or effort for you.

Gardening

Our garden maintenance includes professional and holistic care of your green spaces in order to preserve the beauty, order and health of your outdoor facilities. We offer you a wide range of services that are individually tailored to the needs of your garden.

Our services include regular lawn mowing, hedge trimming, weed removal, planting, fertilization and soil care. In addition, we take care of the professional care of beds, trees and shrubs in order to optimally promote growth and flowering. Seasonal work such as autumn leaf removal and spring planting are also part of our range of services.

We attach great importance to a harmonious design and functional use of your garden areas. We will be happy to advise you on suitable plant species, sustainable irrigation systems and design concepts to transform your property into a true oasis of well-being.

Our goal is to make your outdoor areas not only well-maintained, but also attractive and inviting – whether for a private retreat or as a representative environment for your company.

Property management

Our property management offers you comprehensive and professional maintenance of your properties and outdoor facilities in order to maintain their value, safety and functionality in the long term. We take care of all the care, maintenance and control tasks to ensure the proper use of your buildings.

Our service includes, among other things, regular cleaning, minor repairs, technical checks, green space maintenance and monitoring of the building fabric. In addition, we ensure that building services systems function properly, that minor damage is detected and repaired at an early stage and that your property always remains in optimal condition.

Through regular inspections and preventive measures, we help to avoid major and cost-intensive damage. Our flexible and tailor-made services are aimed at private property owners as well as commercial customers and property managers.

With our professional support, you can concentrate on your core business or your personal use with peace of mind – we will reliably take care of the rest.
